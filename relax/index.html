RELAX

Café & Restaurant, Bar

As you know, there is a lot of nice café, restaurant and bar in vancouver. I will introduce you where I recommend strongly from many place to relax.

Here is the best cafe in Vancouver!!

Nero Waffles

"Nero Waffles" is good for coffee break with some sweets for your treats. And also perfect for brunch. They have some brunch in downtown but I recommend you Seymour Street one.

Breka Bakery & Café

I'm sure that most of people knows "Breka Bakery & Café". Here is my favorite too! Good pastery and sweets, coffee are there! Most important thing is cheap and 24h open. Recommend for student.

Honeys Doughnuts

This is my favorite donut shop ever! "Honeys Doughnuts". Honeys Doughnut is located at DeepCove in North Vancouver. I highly recommend you Honey Doughnut!
